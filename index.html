Our Story

Rooted in craft,
driven by obsession.

Ember & Ground began in a converted garage in 2009, with a secondhand roaster and an unwillingness to compromise. We believed then — as we do now — that exceptional coffee is less about trend and more about patience.

Every bean we source is directly traded, every roast profiled by hand. We visit our farms. We know our growers by name. That relationship is what you taste in every cup.

"The cup is just the end of a very long story — we try to honour every chapter."
— MARCUS OSEI, FOUNDER

From Bean to Cup

The Ember Method

01
Direct Trade

We partner directly with farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Costa Rica — paying above fair-trade prices and visiting annually.

02
Profile Roasting

Each origin is roasted on a custom profile, dialled in over dozens of test batches to unlock its full potential.

03
Precision Brewing

Temperature, grind, dose, and yield are calibrated daily. No guessing — just consistency built on obsessive attention.

04
Served to You

Your cup leaves the bar within 90 seconds of brewing — because coffee at its peak shouldn't wait for anyone.
